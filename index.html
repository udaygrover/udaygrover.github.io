The Open Arms Project

ARMS : "Accommodate, Respect and Mainstream by removing Social barriers".

The project aims to create awareness about Inclusive education in schools.

My contribution In this project:

I with my team have developed a platform where anyone can donate money sitting in any corner of the country to different organisations which we have collaborated with.
As after the pandemic came these organisations which were completely dependent on grants and donations and were not only economically suffering but also all their social ties were broken.
The pandemic not only made them aware about the technical upgradation that was required but also made them worry about their sustainability.
Then we the Open ARMS Team came up with this platform to support them.Not only it was just this much but also to ensure that money or stuff that is required reaches in right hands safely the Open ARMS team is performing audits at regular intervals.
